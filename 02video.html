I started with a client (the Mobile Video team) and market branding survey to best address an enterprise-wide redesign of the branding and presentation standards and templates for Advance.

I started by taking the clients needs to address a diverse set of scenarios involving length of the videos as well as theme of content and need for consistent and easy to apply branding. I then built a basic framework to find the ideal space for content, taking into account the platform demands of YouTube Shorts, TikTok and Instagram.

I developed wireframes and then prototypes that would work within multiple content scenarios for all Advance Digital sites with optimized branding and logo standards. It was also important that the templates have room for some engaging intro's and outro's.

I produced the final templates so they could be used by all sites in either Adobe Premiere Pro or Canva without any obvious differences in the finished product. Finally, I produced collateral training materials to help the client and end-users.

I worked very hard to present the information in an engaging and compelling way.
