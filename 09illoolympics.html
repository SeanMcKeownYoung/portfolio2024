We quickly needed a set of ads, print and digital, to promote our ongoing Olympic coverage. We had already launched ads promoting our Olympic special section. I anticipated that the target customer would need to remember the ad because there was a good chance they wouldn't immediately click but would do so later, when they remembered we had indepth coverage. I wanted the illustration to be elegant, memorable and intriguing. I didn't want it to feel too much like standard Olympics branding.
