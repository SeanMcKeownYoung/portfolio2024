About the portfolio

2023 was a year of growth for me. I completed the Ohio State Bootcamp course in UX-UI in May. From sprint onward, you can see the impact of that education on my work as it became increasingly digital. I've included a few traditional page designs and illustrations to my portfolio, but overall the work is heavily digital in nature.
