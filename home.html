About the portfolio

2024 was a challenging year. Due to major family issues, much of the work that I did this year required me to be flexible and creative. I spent much of the spring working on hospital wifi to finesse a solid collection of Marketing templates. My work continues to evolve primarily toward a digital platform with some connections to print.
We received positive metrics from our Marketing/ECAD partnership efforts. In Q1 we converted 919 NJ subscribers to digital and secured 104 new starts.
